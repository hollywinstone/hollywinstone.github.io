Hi, I'm Holly winstone.

I'm a graphic design student originating from England, currently doing an internship with O'Neill in The Netherlands whilst living in Amsterdam. After immersing myself in art (and paint, glue, ink, pencil...) at school and college I decided graphic design was the world I belonged to. I am soon to be starting my third and final year at the University of Leeds, on course to graduate with a 2:1.

I am interested in the relationship between words and images, whatever form that may take - typography, magazines, collage, digital form etc. I explore my design style in my collages and zines and surround myself with inspiration (blue tack and wall space is my best friend). Blogs, books and magazines absorb my attention where I stare in wonder at the design and broaden my knowledge in [some cases] weirdly wonderful subjects.
